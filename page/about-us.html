ABOUT US

I.T Computer was established in 2004 as a small business with secondhand computers. By having a clear vision and knowledge of the supply and high demands of computers in Cambodia, with continues hard-work and market research we tried to source more products from other countries such as Thailand, Malaysia and Singapore as a whole sale. In 2009, ASUS approached I.T Computer and authorized to distribute their Notebooks, since ASUS had little to none market to becoming the top computer brand in Cambodia. In2015, I.T Computer was also offered the chance to become an authorized distributor by MSI Notebooks. In 2017, ASUS gave us the rights to distribute another product line (Components). In 2019, MSI gave us the rights to distribute MSI Components. In 2020, I.T Computer have partnered with Lenovo (Commercial) as a distributor. I.T Computer has seen rapid growth due to the management of two founders. Currently, I.T Computer is one of the leading companies in distributing computer products(NB, Desktop, Components, and accessories) and services in Cambodia.
